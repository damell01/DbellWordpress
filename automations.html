What We Automate

Our Business Automation Services

We automate the processes that slow your business down and drain your team's energy.

Lead Follow-Up & Nurturing

Instantly send personalized emails and texts to new leads. Set up multi-step nurture sequences that run automatically until a lead converts.

How we do it: We integrate your CRM with email and SMS tools like GoHighLevel, ActiveCampaign, or HubSpot and build multi-step automated sequences that trigger the moment a lead enters your pipeline.

Appointment & Booking Automation

Let clients book online, receive confirmations, and get automated reminders. Zero back-and-forth, zero no-shows.

How we do it: We connect tools like Calendly, Acuity, or custom booking flows that sync with your calendar — automatically sending confirmations and pre-appointment reminders by email and SMS.

Invoicing & Payment Automation

Auto-generate and send invoices when a job is completed. Send reminders for unpaid invoices and reconcile payments automatically.

How we do it: We link your project management or CRM tool to platforms like QuickBooks, FreshBooks, or Stripe — triggering invoices on job completion with smart payment reminder sequences.

AI Chat & SMS Bots

Deploy intelligent chatbots on your website and via SMS that qualify leads, answer FAQs, and book appointments around the clock.

How we do it: We build custom AI-powered chatbots using platforms like ManyChat, GoHighLevel, or custom OpenAI integrations — trained on your business info to qualify leads and handle inquiries 24/7.

Automated Reporting

Receive automated daily, weekly, or monthly performance reports delivered straight to your inbox. Always stay informed without lifting a finger.

How we do it: We connect your data sources — Google Analytics, CRM, ad platforms — to reporting dashboards using Looker Studio or Supermetrics, then set up automated scheduled delivery.

Multi-Platform Data Sync

Automatically sync data between your CRM, email platform, accounting software, and e-commerce store with zero manual updates.

How we do it: We use integration platforms like Zapier, Make (Integromat), or custom API code to create seamless real-time syncs between all your business tools — no manual exporting or copy-pasting.

HR & Employee Onboarding

Automate new hire onboarding, document collection, policy acknowledgments, and team welcome notifications.

How we do it: We build onboarding workflows using platforms like Monday.com, Notion, or HR tools — automatically dispatching documents, tasks, and welcome messages the moment a new hire is added.

E-Commerce Automation

Order confirmations, shipping notifications, abandoned cart recovery, inventory alerts, and customer review requests — all automated.

How we do it: We integrate your Shopify or WooCommerce store with email platforms and automation tools to trigger the right message at every stage of the customer journey — automatically.

Review & Reputation Management

Automatically request reviews from happy customers after every completed job to build your online reputation on autopilot.

How we do it: We set up post-job automation workflows that send review request emails and SMS at the right moment — routing happy customers to Google or Yelp and privately capturing any negative feedback.
