Common Questions About Our Pricing

Hosting is not bundled into the one-time package price — this keeps your costs lower long-term. We'll guide you to a reliable, affordable hosting provider (typically $10–$20/month) and handle the entire setup for you.

No long-term contracts and no surprise monthly fees from us. You pay once for the website build. Optional ongoing maintenance or SEO plans are available but entirely your choice.

The Starter package is typically delivered within 2 weeks. The Business Website takes 3–4 weeks. Custom/Advanced projects vary based on scope and are discussed during our discovery call.

The Starter is a developer-managed site — perfect if you just need a solid online presence and don't need to update it yourself. The Business Website includes a simple CMS so you can edit content, add blog posts, and manage your site independently.
