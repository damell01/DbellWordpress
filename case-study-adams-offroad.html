Case Study

Transforming an Offroad Retailer's Online Presence

Adams Offroad LLC is a Daphne, Alabama-based retailer specializing in offroad accessories, lift kits, tires, and custom builds. Despite a loyal local customer base, they had virtually no digital presence — no website, no Google Business Profile, and no way for new customers to find them online.

DBell Creations stepped in to build a complete online presence from the ground up, resulting in dramatic improvements in leads, traffic, and local search visibility.

Web Design SEO Optimization PHP Contact Form Google Maps API

The Challenge

  • No website or online presence whatsoever
  • Losing customers to competitors with established websites
  • No way for customers to browse products or get information online
  • Zero search engine visibility for local offroad searches
  • No contact form — all inquiries relied on word-of-mouth

Our Solution

  • Custom responsive website built from scratch with bold, rugged branding
  • Full product showcase with categories, descriptions, and photography
  • PHP contact forms for quote requests and customer inquiries
  • Google Maps integration for easy directions to the shop
  • On-page SEO targeting local offroad and automotive keywords
