About The Move Coastal

Gulf Coast Moving Company — Daphne, AL

Case Study

A Moving Company That Needed to Move Online

About The Move Coastal is a full-service moving company based in Daphne, AL, serving customers throughout Baldwin and Mobile Counties along the Gulf Coast. They offer local residential moves, long distance relocations, business moves, and junk removal.

They came to DBell Creations needing a professional web presence that matched their quality of service — something that would build trust instantly and turn website visitors into booked customers.

The Work

Challenge & Solution

⚠️

The Challenge

  • No web presence — losing customers to competitors with established online visibility
  • Moving industry is trust-driven; first impressions matter enormously
  • Needed to clearly communicate multiple service categories without overwhelming visitors
  • Mobile users dominate local service searches — site had to perform perfectly on phones
  • Competitive market in Baldwin County with national moving chains in the mix
✅

The Solution

  • Bold, trust-first design with professional hero section and strong brand identity
  • Prominent phone number and "Get a Free Quote" CTAs above the fold on every page
  • Dedicated service sections for local moves, long distance, business relocations, and junk removal
  • Mobile-first responsive build — fast, clean, and thumb-friendly
  • Local SEO structure targeting "moving company Daphne AL," "Gulf Coast movers," and related terms

Design Approach

Built to Convert, Not Just Look Good

In the moving industry, customers are often anxious and time-pressured. The site was designed to reduce friction and inspire confidence at every scroll — from the hero section straight through to the contact form.

  • Dark, premium aesthetic

    Dark background with gold accents positions the brand as professional and trustworthy — not just another budget mover.

  • Persistent contact CTAs

    Phone number and "Get a Free Quote" button visible throughout the page — no hunting for contact info.

  • Social proof section

    Customer reviews and trust signals prominently featured to reduce hesitation and build confidence fast.

  • Service area clarity

    Explicit coverage of Daphne, Fairhope, Foley, Gulf Shores, Mobile, and the surrounding Gulf Coast area.
