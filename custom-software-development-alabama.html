Case Study

Custom Work Order System — Fasteners & Connectors

The Challenge

Fasteners & Connectors, an Alabama industrial supplier, was managing hundreds of work orders manually with spreadsheets and paper forms. The process was slow, error-prone, and made it nearly impossible to track job status in real time.

The Solution

DBell Creations built a fully custom web-based work order management system. Technicians can create, assign, update, and close work orders from any device. Managers get a real-time dashboard showing job status, technician workloads, and completion metrics.

The Results

  • Work order processing time reduced by over 60%
  • Zero lost work orders since launch
  • Team saves 10+ hours per week previously spent on manual tracking
  • Customer satisfaction scores improved significantly
