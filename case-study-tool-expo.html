Case Study

Full Redesign & SEO for a Mobile, AL Tool Retailer

Tool Expo is a Mobile, Alabama-based retailer specializing in professional-grade tools, equipment, and industrial supplies. Despite carrying a wide inventory, their outdated website was failing to generate meaningful online sales and was effectively invisible in Google search results.

DBell Creations delivered a complete website redesign paired with a robust SEO and digital marketing strategy that moved Tool Expo to the first page of Google and grew their online revenue significantly.

Web Design SEO Strategy E-Commerce Google Ads Digital Marketing

The Challenge

  • Outdated website with poor user experience and slow load times
  • Not appearing on Google for any target tool or equipment keywords
  • Losing e-commerce sales to big-box stores and national competitors
  • No product catalog organization — customers couldn't find what they needed
  • No digital marketing strategy or paid advertising presence

Our Solution

  • Full website redesign with modern, mobile-first layout and fast performance
  • Organized product catalog with categories, filters, and detailed listings
  • Comprehensive SEO strategy targeting high-value local and national keywords
  • Google Shopping integration to compete directly with big-box retailers
  • Google Ads and digital marketing campaigns to drive consistent traffic and sales
