Over 90% of diners look up a restaurant online before walking through the door — checking your menu, your hours, your photos, and your reviews. DBell Creations builds mobile-first restaurant websites engineered to convert hungry searchers into paying guests. The Problem

Why Most Restaurant Websites Hurt More Than Help

Not Mobile-Friendly

The majority of restaurant searches happen on smartphones. If your menu is a PDF, your site takes forever to load, or buttons are too small to tap — your guests give up and find somewhere else to eat.

Invisible on Google Maps

Your competition is ranking on Google and Google Maps for searches like "restaurants near me" and you're not. A properly optimized website — paired with a complete Google Business Profile — is the key to showing up first.

No Online Menu

Customers decide where to eat by browsing menus online. If your menu isn't on your website — or if it's a static, unsearchable PDF — you're losing diners before they ever call or walk in.

What We Build

Restaurant Website Features That Drive Revenue

Digital Menu Integration

A mobile-responsive, easy-to-browse digital menu that loads instantly. No more PDFs. We build menus with categories, item descriptions, pricing, dietary icons (GF, vegan, etc.), and beautiful food photography.

Reservation Widget & Booking System

Accept reservations directly from your website 24/7 — no phone tag required. We integrate OpenTable-style booking, direct reservation forms, or platforms like Resy and Yelp Reservations, all synced with your calendar.

Online Ordering & Delivery Links

Link or embed ordering through DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub, or a custom direct-order solution. We make it easy for guests to order for delivery or pickup directly from your site — keeping more revenue in your pocket.

Food Photography Gallery

Stunning photo galleries that showcase your dishes, ambiance, and team. High-quality visuals are the single most effective way to turn a browsing visitor into a guest who walks through your door.

Event Calendar & Private Dining

Promote your specials, live music nights, holiday events, and private dining packages. We build event calendars and event inquiry forms so you can fill your floor on slow nights and book out private events.

Reviews & Google Business Optimization

Display your 5-star reviews front and center. We integrate Google and Yelp review feeds, add schema markup for rich results, and help you optimize your Google Business Profile to dominate local search.
