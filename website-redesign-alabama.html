Our Process

The DBell Redesign Process

A redesign isn't just a new coat of paint — it's a strategic rebuild. Here's how we approach it.

1. Site Audit & Analysis

We audit your current site for speed, SEO, UX, mobile performance, and conversion rates — identifying exactly what needs to change and why.

2. Strategy & Architecture

We map the new site structure for optimal user journeys and SEO — ensuring every page has a purpose and leads toward conversion.

3. Visual Redesign

New modern design crafted to match your brand identity — clean, professional, and built for the next 5 years, not the last 5.

4. Development & SEO

Rebuilt from scratch for speed and performance — with full SEO optimization, schema markup, and content migration included.

5. Testing & QA

Thorough cross-device, cross-browser testing. We check every form, link, image, and interaction before launch.

6. Launch & Monitor

We handle the go-live process, set up 301 redirects, monitor for issues, and report results in the weeks after launch.
