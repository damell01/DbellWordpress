Website Redesign

Is Your Website Losing You Business?

An outdated, slow, or poorly designed website doesn't just look bad — it actively costs you customers. Studies show that 75% of users judge a company's credibility based on its website design. If your site isn't modern, fast, and mobile-friendly, visitors leave within seconds and go to a competitor.

DBell Creations specializes in website redesigns for Alabama businesses. We transform outdated websites into high-performing digital assets that rank on Google, load fast, and convert visitors into leads and customers.

Based in Fairhope, we've helped businesses across Alabama — including Baldwin County, Mobile, Daphne, Spanish Fort, and Gulf Shores — modernize their online presence and dramatically improve their digital results. Every redesign includes full SEO migration to protect and improve your existing rankings.

Is It Time?

Signs Your Alabama Website Needs a Redesign

Not Mobile-Friendly

Over 60% of web traffic comes from mobile devices. If your site doesn't work well on smartphones, you're losing more than half your potential customers — and Google is penalizing your rankings.

Slow Load Times

A 1-second delay in page load time reduces conversions by 7%. Slow websites drive visitors away and rank lower on Google. Our redesigns include comprehensive performance optimization.

Outdated Design

If your website looks like it was built in 2010, customers notice — and they question whether your business is still current, reliable, and professional. First impressions matter enormously online.

Not Generating Leads

A website should be your best salesperson — working 24/7 to attract, educate, and convert customers. If yours isn't generating regular inquiries, it's time for a conversion-focused redesign.

Poor Google Rankings

If you can't find your own website on Google for your core services, your site has serious technical SEO problems. Our redesigns fix all technical SEO issues to improve your visibility.

Hard to Update

If updating your website requires a developer every time, you're overpaying and moving too slowly. We build on modern platforms that let you (or anyone on your team) make updates easily.
