Case Study

A Full Custom Platform for a Dumpster Rental Business

Trash Panda Roll-Offs is a dumpster rental company serving Baldwin County, Alabama. Before DBell Creations, they were managing bookings manually — phone calls, spreadsheets, and sticky notes tracking which units were out, when they were due back, and who owed what.

We built them a complete two-sided digital platform: a mobile-first customer booking portal where clients can browse available units, select dates, and confirm their rental online — and a full admin backend for the business to manage every aspect of operations from one place.

Custom Software Online Booking Portal Admin Dashboard Inventory Management Invoicing & Payments

The Challenge

  • All bookings handled by phone — no online presence or self-service
  • Inventory tracked in spreadsheets — easy to double-book or lose track of units
  • No invoicing system — billing was manual and slow
  • Work orders for deliveries and pickups were written on paper
  • No visibility into revenue, active rentals, or today's schedule at a glance

Our Solution

  • Mobile-first customer booking portal with real-time unit availability
  • Multi-unit selection and date-range booking with live pricing
  • Full admin backend: bookings, work orders, calendar, and customer management
  • Invoicing and payment tracking built directly into the platform
  • Live dashboard showing active rentals, today's pickups, and available inventory

Customer-Facing

Online Booking Portal

The customer-facing booking portal is a clean, mobile-first web app that lets customers browse available dumpster units, choose their rental dates, and confirm a booking in under two minutes — no phone call required.

Unit Selection
Customers see all available units with size, ID, price, and real-time availability status.
Date Range Picker
Pick start and end dates — the system automatically calculates the rental duration and total price.
Live Booking Summary
An itemized summary shows selected units, dates, and grand total before the customer confirms.
Drop-off Details
Customers enter their address, city, and special delivery instructions — all captured automatically.

Admin Backend

Full Business Management Platform

The admin backend gives the Trash Panda team complete control over every aspect of the business from a single, organized dashboard. No more spreadsheets, sticky notes, or missed pickups.

Dashboard Overview
At-a-glance stats: active work orders, today's pickups, available dumpsters, and revenue.
Bookings Management
View, edit, and manage all customer rentals in one place — past, active, and upcoming.
Work Orders
Create and track delivery and pickup work orders — with real-time status and notification badges.
Calendar View
Visual schedule showing all active and upcoming rentals, deliveries, and pickups by date.
Invoicing & Payments
Generate invoices, track payment status, and run financial reports — all inside the platform.
Inventory Management
Track every dumpster unit by ID, size, and status — always know what's available, deployed, or due back.
